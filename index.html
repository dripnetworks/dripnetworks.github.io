What is Drip Network?

Drip Network is a DeFi platform that aims to make DeFi more user-friendly and accessible for everyone. The platform is built on top of the Ethereum network and offers a range of features and benefits for its users.

Staking

Decentralized Exchange (DEX)

Drip Network has a decentralized exchange (DEX) that allows users to trade their tokens without the need for a centralized exchange. The DEX uses an automated market maker (AMM) algorithm to determine the price of assets in the liquidity pools.
